This application is invented and developed by three students of BCIT CST. The purpose of this report is to explain our application. The programmers of names are Taehyuk, Chris, Troy. Usually, students who will start a new semester are nervous to encounter strange environments. It is not an exception for a student who has a lot of experience for school because of a variety of reasons. To relieve anxiety, students try to find information related to a new class, school, or courses. It would be the Internet, friends, alumni, or parents, and it would usually be the Internet. The problem is that there is no proper website for students since it is spread out all over the Internet world. That is why our application was invented. Our application provides a variety of information such as a board, calendar, etc, and it is easy to access and find. They do not have to jump on the website to get it if they use our application. We will explain our application by using Ben Shneidermans 8 Golden Rules. Shneiderman's eight golden rules are intended to help designers solve problems, and we totally use these instructions. Having clear guidelines will allow us to orientate our audits and will allow us to detect the main problems that we may encounter on our web interfaces.
